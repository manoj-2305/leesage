Order Processing & Tracking

Order Processing

Orders are processed Monday through Friday, excluding holidays. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will begin processing the next business day.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package using:

  • Our Order Tracking page
  • Carrier websites (USPS, FedEx, UPS)
  • Our mobile app (coming soon)

Delivery Issues

If your package is delayed or lost, please contact us within 30 days of the expected delivery date. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue promptly.

Returns & Exchanges

  • 30-day return policy from delivery date
  • Free returns on your first return per order
  • Prepaid return label provided

Special Shipping Considerations

Holiday Shipping

During peak holiday seasons, shipping times may be extended. We recommend ordering early to ensure timely delivery. Holiday shipping deadlines will be posted on our website.

Weather Delays

Severe weather conditions may cause shipping delays beyond our control. We'll notify you of any significant delays and work to get your order to you as quickly as possible.

Signature Required

High-value orders may require a signature upon delivery for security purposes. If you're unavailable, the carrier will leave a notice with instructions for redelivery or pickup.
