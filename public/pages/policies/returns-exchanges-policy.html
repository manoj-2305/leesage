How to Return

Online Returns

  1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the order and items you want to return
  3. Choose your reason for return
  4. Select refund or exchange option
  5. Print your prepaid return label
  6. Pack items securely in original packaging
  7. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

In-Store Returns

Bring your item with original receipt and tags to any Lee Sage retail location. Our team will process your return immediately.
